How russian propagandists change their rhetoric after 24.02.2022?

A small research of russian propagandists telegram channels before and after 24.02.2022. How did they change their rhetorics if they did? How often do they use "newspeak". List of "newspeak" according to newspeak list

First step

To begin with, lets parce russian propagandists telegram chats history before and after beginning "special military operation" further "SMO", lets count 30 most frequently using words before and after.

For a convenience I made a translation to english via deep_translator. Lets have a look:

Vladimir Soloviev

26.05.2019 till 24.02.2022 "Ukraine" has been mentioned 6616 times. After 24.02.2022 "Ukraine" and "ukrainian" in sum have been mentioned 13505 times till 18.11.2022. For incomplete 9 months the increase is about 204%! Also I want to note, that from Vladimir Soloviev's rhetorics completely disappeared all mentioning about Alexei Navalny (6171 before "SMO" started), coronavirus (6600). Mention frequency of russian president Vladimir Putin dicreased from 11th place in a list of top-30 most frequency mentioned words before "SMO" started to 24th. New words in Vladimir Soloviev's top-30 most frequency words, indicated with a dash: "war - 2183", "territory - 2124", "operation - 2018", "strike - 1993", "destroy - 1850".

Ramzan Kadyrov

New words in Ramzan Kadyrov's top-30 most frequency words, indicated with a dash: "Ukraine - 336", "hero - 229", "help - 208", "ukrainian - 190", "power - 188", "task - 183". I would especially like to point out that, words "life - 186", "growth - 184", "new - 183" disappeared. Words "world/peace - 315", "Putin - 278", were changed respectfully to "peaceful - 183", "president - 270".

Olga Skabeeva

23.11.2017 till 24.02.2022 "Ukraine" has been mentioned 394 times. After 24.02.2022 "Ukraine" and "ukrainian" in sum have been mentioned 640 times till 18.11.2022. For incomplete 9 months the increase is about 162%! Also, from Olga Skabeeva's rhetorics completely disappeared all mentioning about coronavirus (247 before "SMO" started), Trump (321). But there were mentions of the following words: indicated with a dash: "Zelenskyy - 119", "negotiations - 98", "Kyiv - 94", "Ministry of Defense - 94", "enjoy - 89", "power - 78".
