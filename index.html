How russian propagandists change their rhetoric after 24.02.2022?

To begin with, lets parce russian propagandists telegram chats history before and after beginning "special military operation" further "SMO". Second step: lets count 30 most frequently using words before and after.

Vladimir Soloviev

26.05.2019 till 24.02.2022 "Ukraine" has been mentioned 6616 times. After 24.02.2022 "Ukraine" and "ukrainian" in sum have been mentioned 13505 times till 18.11.2022. For incomplete 9 months the increase is about 204%! Also I want to note, that from Vladimir Soloviev's rhetorics completely disappeared all mentioning about Alexei Navalny (6171 before "SMO" started), coronavirus (6600). Mention frequency of russian president Vladimir Putin dicreased from 11th place in a list of top-30 most frequency mentioned words before "SMO" started to 24th. New words in Vladimir Soloviev's top-30 most frequency words, indicated with a dash: "destroy - 1850", "strike - 1993", "war - 2183", "operation - 2018", "territory - 2124".
